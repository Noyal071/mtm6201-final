Featured Program and News Highlights

We're excited to announce the launch of our Community Wellness Series, a monthly program focused on promoting mental and physical health for all ages. Each session includes expert-led workshops on nutrition, stress management, and low-impact fitness, tailored for beginners and seniors.

In June, we hosted a successful Mind & Movement day that brought together over 100 community members for guided yoga, mindfulness sessions, and healthy cooking demos. Another cornerstone of the series is the Senior Strength & Balance workshop, designed specifically for older adults to build strength and improve stability through safe, low-impact exercises.
